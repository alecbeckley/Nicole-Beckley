About Me

--------------------

I started modelling when I was 15. All throughout my life I have always been interested in the arts, whether that be acting on stage, sketching on paper, painting on a canvas, or poses for a great photo. I won my first modelling competition which included photoshoots as well as a runway show in 2016 in a competition in the Philippines called "100 Cutest Faces". Shortly after, I starred in a few short commercials for Palmolive. I am still continuing to model and having fun doing it.
